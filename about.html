Audu Matthew Ogiede

From humble beginnings in Northern Nigeria to becoming a powerhouse in real estate and business, Audu Matthew's journey is one of determination, excellence, and groundbreaking success.

Born in Kano State to Engr. Audu Oyakere Matthew and Mrs. Angela Chioma Audu, Audu Matthew Jr. is the 7th child in a family of 8. His diverse heritage from Etsako LGA in Edo State and Owerri North LGA in Imo State has shaped his unique perspective on business and life.

Core Strengths

  • Sales Mastery: Record-breaking sales performance
  • Leadership: Guiding teams to exceptional results
  • Vision: Building enterprises that create impact

Key Milestones In His Journey

1988

Born in Kano State to Engr. Audu Oyakere Matthew and Mrs. Angela Chioma Audu.

Early Education

Started nursery education at Hamdala Continental School in Kano State.

Relocation

Moved to Agenebode, Edo State during the Kano riots and continued education at Father Piotin Memorial School.

Lagos Education

Completed education in Lagos, attaining a B.Sc in Accounting from the University of Lagos.

Professional Career

Worked at Cams Consult as an Auditor and Dream Empire Music Label as its first Account Officer.

Real Estate Journey

Joined Property World African Network as a Sales Consultant, quickly becoming the highest sales person.

2023/2024

Achieved highest sales record at PWAN Champion and was gifted a brand new Hyundai Santefe.

Leadership Role

Rose to Managing Director/CEO of PWAN Ultra Estates and Realtors Ltd.

Entrepreneurship

Founded Damains Enterprises, focusing on Real Estate Development, Interior Decoration, Fashion, Agriculture, and Consultancy.

2025

Awarded at the Under 40's CEO's Conference in Johannesburg, South Africa for outstanding performance in Real Estate.

Family Life

Married to Mrs. Joyce Imoiseme Audu, building a beautiful home and family together.
